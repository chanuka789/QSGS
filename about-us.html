Who We Are

Redefining Precision and Efficiency in Quantity Surveying

We specialize in remote quantity surveying across multiple sectors including commercial, residential, mixed-use, hotels, healthcare, educational, and infrastructure projects.

Our organization was established by seasoned construction professionals addressing global demand for reliable and accurate surveying services that could transcend geographical boundaries.

Our core services include cost management, BOQ preparation, and precise take-offs utilizing advanced technology. We serve property developers, consultant firms, contractors, and independent architects worldwide.

What We Offer

Our Core Services

We offer 2D and 3D take-off services along with BoQ preparation for Architectural, Structural, Mechanical, Electrical (M&E) & Plumbing, Infrastructural, Landscape, Irrigation, ICT and Security systems & Data centers.

Take-Off Quantities

Precise take-off quantities for construction projects using 2D drawings and 3D models to support cost estimation and project planning.

BOQ Preparation

Complete Bills of Quantities with precise quantities, adhering to drawings, specifications, and standard methods of measurement.

Cost Management

Comprehensive cost estimation and management services utilizing advanced technology and industry expertise.
