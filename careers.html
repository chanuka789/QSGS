Quantity Surveyor (Remote)

Full-Time Part-Time Contract

Key Responsibilities

  • ✓ Perform accurate quantity take-offs from drawings and models (2D & 3D)
  • ✓ Prepare detailed Bills of Quantities (BoQs) adhering to standard methods of measurement
  • ✓ Collaborate with project stakeholders
  • ✓ Maintain software proficiency across industry tools
  • ✓ Ensure compliance with measurement standards
  • ✓ Maintain clear client communication

Requirements

  • ✓ Bachelor's degree in Quantity Surveying or Construction Management
  • ✓ Minimum 2 years professional experience
  • ✓ Proficiency in Revit, Bluebeam, PlanSwift, AutoCAD
  • ✓ Knowledge of NRM, SMM, CESMM standards
  • ✓ Strong analytical and detail-oriented capabilities
  • ✓ Remote team collaboration skills
